The Problem

Great products die every day.
Not because they were bad.

Because nobody found them. Distribution failure looks exactly like product failure. Most founders quit the right product for the wrong reason. We are here to make sure that never happens to you.

01
You posted on Reddit. Got removed.
You found a relevant subreddit, shared your product, and got banned or ignored. Reddit is not a megaphone. It is a community. There is a right way to use it and most founders never figure it out.
02
You posted content everywhere. Crickets.
You posted on LinkedIn. Tweeted into the void. Nothing happened. Not because your product is bad. Because the message was off and the channel was wrong.
03
Your ProductHunt launch flopped.
ProductHunt rewards products that already have believers. It is not a discovery platform. It is amplification. You needed a crowd before you launched, not after. Most founders get this backwards.

Answer Engine Optimisation

Your buyers are
searching on ChatGPT.

When someone asks an AI "best tool for X" your product either shows up or it does not. AEO is what SEO was in 2010. The founders who move early will own their category before everyone else figures out it matters.

Reddit presenceReddit is heavily indexed by AI right now. If your competitor gets recommended in relevant threads and you do not, that is exactly why they show up in AI answers and you do not.
Third-party mentionsAI trusts what other people say about you, not what you say about yourself. Reviews on G2, Capterra, ProductHunt all feed AI training data. Your own website saying you are great means almost nothing to it.
Directory listingsG2, Capterra, AlternativeTo, SaaSHub. Every listing is a data point AI pulls from when answering questions about your category. We get you listed everywhere that matters.
Founder authorityWhen you show up consistently as a voice in your problem space, AI starts associating your name and product with that category. Founder visibility is an AEO signal most people completely ignore.

FAQ

Questions founders
always ask us.

Do you work with pre-launch founders? +
Honestly, no. We need a real product to distribute. If you are still building, bookmark us and come back when you are live. We will be here.
How is TDL different from a regular marketing agency? +
Most agencies do a bit of everything and nothing particularly well. We do one thing. Organic distribution for early-stage SaaS on Reddit, LinkedIn, and Twitter. That focus is why we are better at it than anyone trying to do everything.
How long before I see results? +
Honest answer: first real conversations usually start in week 2 or 3. First users show up around week 3 or 4. Month 3 is always stronger than month 1 because distribution compounds. That is why the pilot converts to a retainer.
What if my product is very niche? +
Niche is actually better for us. The more specific your ICP, the easier it is to find exactly where they hang out online. Broad products are hard to distribute organically. Niche products have tight communities and we find them every time.
Do you need access to our product? +
Yes. We need to understand what you built deeply enough to talk about it honestly. Surface-level distribution is obvious and it does not convert. Give us access and we will represent your product the right way.
Can you guarantee a number of users? +
No. And honestly, any agency that guarantees specific numbers is lying to you. What we guarantee is consistent, high-quality distribution work on the right channels with the right message. Results follow from that. We have never had a client see zero traction.
Do you work with B2C SaaS? +
Our channels are native to B2B. If your product is consumer-facing, TDL is probably not the right fit and we would rather tell you that upfront. We are built for SaaS founders selling to businesses and professionals.
What is AEO and why does it matter? +
AEO means making sure your product shows up when someone asks ChatGPT or Perplexity "best tool for X". It is what SEO was in 2010. Founders who build these signals now will own their category in AI search before their competitors even know it is a thing. We build AEO into everything we do.
How many clients do you work with at once? +
Maximum 5 retainer clients at any time. That is intentional. Good distribution work needs real focus. We would rather go deep on fewer products and actually move the needle than spread thin and deliver mediocre results everywhere.
What happens after the 30-day pilot? +
You get a full report of everything we did, what worked, and what the next 60 days should look like. If you want to continue, great. If not, no hard feelings and you keep everything we built. Zero pressure.
